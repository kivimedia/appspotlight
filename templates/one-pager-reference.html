The Problem

Choir members forget their parts between rehearsals

Community choir singers meet once a week and spend precious rehearsal time re-learning songs they already practiced. Directors repeat the same sections over and over. Members feel embarrassed when they cannot remember their vocal part, and some stop showing up entirely. There is no good tool for practicing Hebrew choral music at home.

The Solution

An AI vocal coach that lives in your pocket

ChoirMind gives every choir member a personal practice companion. It knows your choir's repertoire, isolates your vocal part, and provides AI-powered feedback on pitch and timing. Built Hebrew-first for the Israeli community choir ecosystem, it transforms the time between rehearsals into productive practice sessions.

What It Does

Four pillars of better
choir practice

🎧

Smart Part Isolation

Select your vocal part (soprano, alto, tenor, bass) and hear it isolated from the full arrangement. Practice your line with or without the other parts playing softly in the background for context.

🤖

AI Vocal Coaching

Record yourself singing and get instant feedback on pitch accuracy, timing, and breath support. The AI coach identifies specific measures where you need more practice and suggests targeted exercises.

📚

Hebrew-First Song Library

A growing library of Hebrew choral arrangements with full RTL support, transliterations for new learners, and synchronized lyrics that highlight each word as the music plays. Directors can upload custom arrangements.

📈

Progress Dashboard

Track your practice streaks, see which songs you have mastered, and identify where you need more work. Directors get a birds-eye view of their entire choir's readiness before each rehearsal.
